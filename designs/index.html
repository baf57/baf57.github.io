The following designs were all made with the intent of being 3D printed via FDM methods. This is not an exhaustive list of all my designs, just the ones which were some of the most successful. I jumped around between different CAD software before finally settling on OnShape. Some of these designs were started in SolidWorks and then imported into OhShape when I lost access to SolidWorks.

Car Shifter Knob

This is a shifter knob I designed for my car which has an inset of The Comedian's Badge from the graphic novel Watchmen on it. I have used this extensively over the last 2 years and it has survived in my car without any warping or structural breakdown in temperatures exceeding 110°F.

Bicycle Phone Mount

Small Nintendo Switch Docks

A small foot-print dock for the Nintendo Switch that I made for myself and others to use. It uses the same internal components as the proprietary dock, and the larger design allows for the Switch to be dockable without removing any protective case that is on it.
